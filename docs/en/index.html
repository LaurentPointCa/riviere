About the project

This site displays a 5-day forecast of the water flow and level of the Rivière des Prairies, at hydrometric station 043301 in Laval, Québec.

A machine-learning model (LightGBM) is trained on more than 45 years of historical data from five hydrological stations in Québec and Ontario, combined with climate data (temperature, precipitation, snow). At forecast time, the predicted weather for the next 5 days is injected into the model to produce the displayed values.

The forecast is updated automatically twice a day.

How to read the charts
  • Solid lineObserved values (station historical data)
  • Dashed line with circlesModel forecast for the next 5 days
  • Shaded bandTypical model error margin (±1 RMSE)
  • Dashed line + grey bandHistorical mean and min/max envelope
  • Vertical lineDate of the last observation (start of the forecast)
  • Orange line (2,500 m³/s)Concern threshold
  • Red line (3,000 m³/s / 22.5 m)Near-flood threshold / danger zone
